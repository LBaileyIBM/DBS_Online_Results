Share Result

You can share the result of your Enhanced (or Standard?) DBS check with someone else, like your employer.

How to share your result

To share your result, you will need to send them:

  • a one-time share code
  • the last 6 digits of your certificate number
  • a web link:
    https://www.view-criminal-record-check.service.gov.uk/view-result/employer

When generating a share code you will also receive an email containing the web link and last 6 digits of your certification number

The share code you receive can only be used once
