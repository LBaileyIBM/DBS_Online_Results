View your DBS Online Result

See the results of your application for a DBS check. This application may have been made on your behalf by a third party, such as your employer.

You can use this service to:

  • View the results of your DBS check
  • Share your results with someone else, e.g. your employer
  • Manage who you are sharing your results with
  • Contact DBS if any information on your result is incorrect

What you will need

To view your results, you will need:

  • Your mobile phone - we will text you a security code to the number you provided in the application
  • Your certificate number - find this on the email we sent telling you that your results are ready
  • Your GOV.UK Sign In login details. You will be able to create an account if you do not already have one.
